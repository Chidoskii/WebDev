Hobbies and Interests

Categories

Sound

     Music has been very important to me for as long as I can remember. I first started playing the alto sax in elementary school. Nowadays, I clumsily pluck away at a guitar during my free time. Some artists that inspired me to play the guitar are Bob Marley and Jack Johnson. I like a variety of music, pretty much anything that sounds good, but I mainly listen to rap.

Sports

     My favorite sport is basketball. I got into playing basketball at a young age by participating in many city leagues. These days, I watch a lot of sports and I am interested in the analysis of sports data. One day, I would like to build an app that tracks the stats of every player/team in a league and uses that knowledge base to make reliable predictions on the outcome of a match. Until then, I'll just continue watching UFC events every saturday.

Spirits


"Why is the rum always gone?"            -Sparrow

      Hmmm... How did I get into this? You know, I really couldn't say. But that is not important right now. The rich notes of a quality Bourbon is all that matters now. One day I'll probably take some bartending classes and learn how to mix some award winning cocktails.

Stories

     Everyone loves a good story and I am no different. One of my favorite books to read (I dont read many books) was 'The Series of Unfortunate Events'. There are too many good movies out there for me to have an absolute favorite, but I really like Dead Man's Chest. Again, there are too many good television shows out there, such as: 'Avatar: The Last Airbender', 'Narcos', and 'Game of Thrones'. When it comes to video games, GTA V online and Red Dead Redemption II online always had me pulling all nighters. But the best stories are the ones that are real; the ones that happened in history.

Sustenance

      Food is an integral part of all of our lives. It's quite simple, really. If you dont eat then you better start making preparations for the afterlife. Naturally, I intend on sticking around for a bit longer, so I am always on the hunt for some good food to eat. I am not the greatest cook, but I have a few survival meals that I can prepare if need be. I would like to learn how to cook 5 star meals, but for now pizza from Domino's will suffice.
