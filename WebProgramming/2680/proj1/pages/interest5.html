On Culinary Arts

     Culinary arts are the cuisine arts of food preparaation, cooking, and representation of food. Primitive humans were the first to explore culinary arts when they began cooking their foods with fire. The introduction of tools, domestication of livestock, and advancement in agriculture, improved culinary techniques over time. Culinary arts began to evolve towards the end of the Renaissance period. Before then, chefs typically worked in castles preparing food for Kings, Queens, and other members of the court.

Food and Nutrition

     It is vital for one to manage their food and nutrition intake as it can have a major impact on one's health. Our bodies get the energy they need from food and nutrition. Some of the major areas of nutrition are fats, protein, and carbohydrates. Water is extremely vital to our survival, so it is essential that we are drinking enough water on a daily basis. To ensure that one is eating a healthy diet, a sizable portion of the diet should consists of natural foods like fruits and vegetables. Make sure to eat plenty of lean protein as well. Also, it is very important to avoid certain foods, such as those with high sodium or glucose levels.

Obesity in America

  • Nearly 1 in 3 adults (30.7%) are overweight.
  • More than 2 in 5 adults (42.4%) have obesity.
  • About 1 in 11 adults (9.2%) have severe obesity.
  • About 1 in 6 children and adolescents ages 2 to 19 (16.1%) are overweight.
  • Almost 1 in 5 children and adolescents ages 2 to 19 (19.3%) have obesity.
  • About 1 in 16 children and adolescents ages 2 to 19 (6.1%) have severe obesity.

According to 2017-2018 data from the National Health and Nutrition Examination Survey
